About Jointek Fine Wines

JFW established in 1990's, has over 20 years experience in the hospitality industry and is one the leading importer of international wine brands in the region. Our mission and philosophy is to continue to provide China and Hong Kong wine lovers with high quality, value for money wines from all around the world.

 

Enjoy Life - Enjoy Wines

 

With a commitment to a quality retail experience to our wine lover, over 100 JFW wine shops are distributed all over Taiwan, Singapore, Hong Kong and China. JFW also provides other value added services such as free storage in our 24 hours temperature controlled warehouse, tutored wine tasting session and wine dinner arrangement.
